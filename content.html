Everything You Need to Teach Digital Media Literacy

This site has been created to provide you with resources, plans, and help when teaching media literacy in the classroom. Feel free to explore:


Start with the context page to understand the importance of media literacy and discover the skills that it can provide your students with. Next, the lesson plans will provide you with three developed lesson plans to begin your process of introducing media literacy to the classroom. The resources tab collates videos, articles, and other tools to help expand on the imporatance and relevancy of media literacy, both for you and your students.
